Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any restrictions for playing?

You must be at least 18 years old to play using real money. However, everyone is eligible to play the virtual currency model of our games, as long as they follow the GoProphet rules.

Can I make bets during the actual game?

Yes you can! One of our selling points is that users can make bets during the actual game, thus causing more excitement and more incentive to both play GoProphet and watch the NFL.

What game modes are currently available?

We currently have two game modes on GoProphet, NFL market and Game Mode B. Both games can be played in real time, and offer crowdsourced statistics that can be viewed by any user, free of charge.
